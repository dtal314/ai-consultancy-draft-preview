Stage Two - ADOPT: The 90-Day Ai Immersion

Stage Two includes all the options provided in Stage 1 and then builds an even stronger foundation to move your firm into Ai adoption. It includes more presentations with greater immersion and case studies. Identifying the firms’ biggest adopters and providing targeted, individual training ensures an ai champion, someone who can pursue RnD and maintain firm adoption.

Stage Two introduces the Citizen Developer. The emergent trend of developing software, databases, apps and websites without the need for expertise in coding and enhanced with the understanding of design and process.

Deeper Ai knowledge Scope

Five additional one-hour presentations (total of 10), reinforce Stage One lessons

  • Comprehensive understanding of agents, how they work, when to use them.
  • Making the most of prompts - creating prompting databases and guides
  • Encouraging experimentation in everyday processes, case studies
  • Training on specific tools for modeling, rendering, marketing and modeling
  • Introduction to Citizen Coding: in-house app and software making

Individual Training

  • Identify firm leaders or people to lead up an internal Ai RnD team
  • 1-3 people who get immersive training in the following approaches

Prompt Training

  • Deeper research, how to combine bots, tools and data for idea:
    • Idea generation
    • Problem solving
    • Detailed data driven reports
    • Free thinking to get unexpected results, solve the impossible problems
  • Visualization and generative Ai, for projects, marketing and proposals, BD
    • Learn the various out of the box prompting to generate unexpected products and results that can be client facing
    • Develop custom styles and approach

Citizen Coding Individual

  • Provide Custom tools for marketing, visualization, prompting and other project and firm needs
  • Train individuals on how to approach and create custom tools
  • Creating a secure environment
  • Introduction to Claude Code and ChatGPT Codex
  • Understanding time, costs and attitude
  • Developing a custom tool for the firm
Investment Investment: $20,000-25,000
