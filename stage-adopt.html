Common questions

About this stage.

What is the 90-Day AI Immersion?

The 90-Day AI Immersion is Stage Two of three. It includes everything in Stage One and builds a stronger foundation for adoption: five additional one-hour presentations for ten in total, plus immersive individual training for one to three people identified as the firm's AI champions. It introduces the Citizen Developer. The investment is $20,000 to $25,000.

What is a Citizen Developer?

The Citizen Developer is the emergent trend of developing software, databases, apps and websites without the need for expertise in coding — enhanced by an understanding of design and process. It is the reason a designer, rather than a software engineer, is often the right person to build a firm's internal tools.

Do we have to do Stage One first?

Stage Two includes all the options provided in Stage One. It is built on that foundation rather than replacing it, so the literacy work happens either way.
