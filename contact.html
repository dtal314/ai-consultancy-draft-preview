Common questions

Before you write.

How do I start working with an AI consultant for my design firm?

Bring one specific, repeatable workflow rather than a general interest in AI. Describe what your firm is testing, where it is stuck, and what must remain true — security, client confidentiality, professional liability, or design quality. The first conversation is about whether AI is the right tool for that workflow at all.

Are there free AI sessions for AEC firms?

Yes. Free 30-minute online AI meetups with a 15-minute Q&A are offered, along with local in-person meetups in Denver and Washington DC. They are open to ASLA, AIA, civil and broader AEC audiences and remain free and public. Dates and registration — Dan to provide
