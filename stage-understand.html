About this stage.

What is the 60-Day AI Accelerator?

The 60-Day AI Accelerator is Stage One of three. It spreads AI literacy and understanding to all staff and stakeholders through five one-hour firm presentations, a firm survey of who is already using what, a policy, privacy and security engagement, and twelve months of continuing education resources. The investment is $10,000 to $15,000.

Who is Stage One for?

Owners who know they need to tool up and understand AI — and who want to guide curious staff that are probably already using it. It suits firms looking to go beyond the headlines and social media posts to understand the emergent AI ecosystem and how it impacts their work, finances and life.

What does Stage One cost?

Stage One is $10,000 to $15,000. That estimate reflects 10 to 20 hours of work at $250 to $500 an hour.
