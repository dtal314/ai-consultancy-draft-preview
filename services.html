Common questions

About these services.

What does AI 101 cover?

AI 101 covers the LLM ecosystem of bots, which bots are worth subscriptions and why, general writing with AI including emails, proposals and memos, deep searches on specific topics, general AI literacy for the firm, specific AI literacy for particular people within the firm, and how to organize and keep track of AI use within your firm. It is the base layer everything else sits on.

What is AEO and how is it different from SEO?

AEO is answer engine optimization: structuring a website so AI answer engines can read, understand and cite it. SEO gets you found; AEO gets you quoted. It covers the basic needs that make a website searchable by AI, becoming the proven expert for AI to find, and specific approaches for optimization. The difference matters because people increasingly ask an assistant instead of scanning ten blue links — and the assistant only mentions who it can read.
