Common questions

About this stage.

What is the 180-Day AI Competency?

The 180-Day AI Competency is Stage Three of three. It builds on the Stage One and Stage Two offerings, which are included. It focuses on building a core AI team — AI Technology people, or AiTs — to propel the firm forward with a dedicated R&D person or group. The Citizen Developer is expanded to full potential, and it introduces the more powerful use of agents. The investment is $30,000 to $40,000.

What is an AiT?

An AiT is an AI Technology person — a dedicated in-house R&D person or group given the full scope of current understanding and tools, whose purpose is to propel the firm forward and maintain adoption from the inside.

Does Stage Three include the earlier stages?

Yes. Stage Three builds on the Stage One and Stage Two offerings, and both are included in the 180-day AI Competency.
